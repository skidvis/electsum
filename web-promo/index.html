How It Works

Enter a New Password [offline]

You enter a new password which will be used to secure your details. A SHA-256 hash of the password is created and saved, your password is never stored or transmitted.

Enter Your Electrum Master Public Key [offline]

Enter the Master Public Key that Electrum generated for you. Your mpk is then encrypted using Military-Grade AES encryption, using the password you created, and then only your Public addresses are generated and stored.

Your Balances Are Tallied [online]

At this point, and from now on, the app will communicate with Blockchain.info to get your balances on the addresses, and the local Bitcoin price will be pulled from Coinbase.com to provide you with USD conversions.
